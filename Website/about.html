Mid Career
Risk Manager
Financial Services & Commodities

Moved into risk management — covering portfolio-level exposure across financial and commodity markets, counterparty risk, stress testing, and how institutions protect capital in adverse conditions. Managing risk across commodities added a dimension of volatility and macro sensitivity that equity-only frameworks miss entirely. This gave me a rigorous, multi-asset framework for risk that goes well beyond the standard "high/medium/low" categories most retail investors use.
